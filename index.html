Celeste White

Entrepreneur · Philanthropist · Nonprofit Leader

Celeste White brings together business innovation, public education, and deep community service in Napa Valley. As founder of Lux Forum, CEO of Horse Rock Olive Oil, and a decades-long nonprofit board leader, she demonstrates that purpose-driven enterprise and civic responsibility are not competing values—they are one and the same.

St. Helena, Napa Valley, California

Who She Is

A Life Shaped by Service, Land, and Ideas

Celeste White is a Napa Valley–based entrepreneur and philanthropist whose work spans public education, agricultural enterprise, healthcare innovation, and community leadership. She serves as Founder, President, and Chair of Lux Forum, a thought-leadership organization that brings scholars and cultural leaders into dialogue with local communities throughout the region.

Her connection to the land is equally central to her identity. As CEO of Horse Rock Olive Oil, White produces estate-grown olive oil on her family's ranch near St. Helena—a venture rooted in agricultural stewardship and Napa Valley's heritage of quality, land-based production. She is also co-founder of two healthcare-focused ventures, Stitches Medical and WearTootles.com, reflecting her commitment to compassionate innovation.

A graduate and Trustee of Westmont College, Celeste has devoted decades to nonprofit board service across Northern California, supporting youth development, faith-based initiatives, and organizations including The Salvation Army, Hospice, and Ag 4 Youth. She lives on her St. Helena ranch with her husband, Dr. Robert White.
